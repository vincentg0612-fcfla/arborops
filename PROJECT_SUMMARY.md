# Detailed project summary

ArborOps + Olive is an AI-powered operations platform built specifically for tree service companies.

We started with a generic Odoo 19 foundation and transformed it into a full tree-service operating system. Out of the box, Odoo did not understand estimate visits, route-aware scheduling, crew assignments, weather-sensitive planning, emergency response, or employee-first operational tools. We designed ArborOps to fill that gap.

The result is a custom platform that manages estimates, jobs, work orders, crews, calendar operations, routing context, maintenance workflows, time tracking, attendance, and employee portal experiences. On top of that platform, we built Olive — a conversational AI assistant that uses real business data to help people make decisions and take action.

Olive is available in both the native iPhone app and the manager portal. She can answer daily operational questions, identify scheduling gaps, recommend what should be scheduled next, evaluate weather risk, support route planning, create follow-up tasks, and execute scheduling actions after user confirmation. Instead of forcing managers and crews to jump between ERP screens, weather sites, map tools, notes, and phone calls, Olive provides a single conversational interface into the operating system.

ArborOps also includes a dedicated emergency workflow. A crew member can declare an emergency from an active job, which immediately escalates the incident to managers and foremen with job and crew context. The system can surface emergency contacts, blood type, and pertinent medical information, while also giving the employee immediate access to a 911 call action. This extends the platform beyond operational efficiency into real field safety.

The architecture combines a customized Odoo 19 backend, n8n orchestration, Azure AI for language reasoning, Azure Speech for voice, Open-Meteo for weather and geocoding, and route-aware scheduling logic. The iPhone app and manager portal both connect to the same operating model, giving the assistant a consistent and useful view of the business.

This showcase highlights a core idea: AI becomes genuinely valuable when it is tied directly to the workflows, constraints, safety needs, and data model of a real business. ArborOps + Olive is not a demo chatbot. It is a functioning vertical software product that rethinks how a tree service company can schedule work, manage crews, and operate day to day.
